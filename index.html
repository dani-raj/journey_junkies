Tastes worth taking that long-haul flight

Filipino cuisine is just as rich as its natural world. With their influences throughout their history and their traditional cooking techniques, Filipinos tend to combine flavours and make the most of any ingredients. Our trips not only introduce you to the most beautiful places of the country but also to its most delicious meals. Hungry yet?

An underwater world that takes your breath away

Watching David Attenborough documentaries are nice, but why not make your own discoveries? From the giant whale shark to the tiny clown fish, the Philippines never fails to amaze anyone going underwater. It’s also home to one of the few biggest coral reefs, that you can also explore with us.

Ever wondered what would you bring to a deserted island?

Now is the time to answer that.
Spend a night on a real deserted island and let your worries get washed away as the ocean gently reaches your feet. Our camp awaits you with the comfort of a luxurious hotel and the nomad experience of a thousand years. But before you let your inner Robinson Cruso come alive, let us tell you that meals are also provided by our Michellin-star chef.
